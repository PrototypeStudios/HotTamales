A little bit about Hot Tamales

For over 20 years Hot Tamales has provided El Paso with the best authentic Mexican Food in town. GREAT PLACE FOR AUTHENTIC MEXICAN FOOD

Our food speaks for itself...

20 years in business teaches you a lot of things, for instance you can't expect high quality food if you cut corners. That's why here at Hot Tamales we focus on working hard to provide the best authentic Mexican food in El Paso, TX. We use fresh produce and take our time with each one of our orders, making everything taste real, not processed and stale. Nothing is pre-grilled at our kitchen, as soon as you place your order we begin the cooking, and mold your order, from our grill to your plate. Our goal is put a smile on your face with our authentic Mexican style we do nothing fancy... Just "GOOOOOD".
