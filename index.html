A little bit about Hot Tamales

For years we at Hot Tamales have provided El Paso's east side with some of the best Mexican food around. We try to make things simple. No flashy cooking techniques or recipes, just good ingredients and hard working people putting together delicious Mexican food. So the next time you hear your stomach rumble, get in your car and come find us, we'll make you something that'll surely hit the spot.

Our food speaks for itself:

20 plus years in the restaurant business teaches you quite a few things. Like having mariachis, elaborate fountains, gaudy colors or live animals automatically makes your restaurant great. No it doesn't. You can have all the ambiance in the world but if the food isn't great, you have nothing. That's what sets us apart form other places in town. What we offer is simple, no frills, good Mexican food. Our slogan says it best: "Nothing fancy, just goooood".
