OUR STORY

Serving the community for over 30 years

We first opened our doors in August of 1992 when the eastside of town was mostly sill in its adolescence, but we weren't going in blind. Being the sister location of the lower valley institution La Reina Mexican Foods, we had a starting point. We've seen this side of town grow into what it is today but our product and mission has never waivered. We still provide the same simple and delicious mexican food our family has been known for in teh community for 30 plus years.

"Nothing fancy, just goooood!"
